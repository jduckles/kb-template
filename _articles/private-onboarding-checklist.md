This checklist is for **community leads** walking a new member through their first week. Share the direct URL with new members; it won't appear in the public article index.

## Week 1 Checklist

- [ ] Welcome message sent via Slack
- [ ] Added to GitHub organization
- [ ] Access to shared drives confirmed
- [ ] Intro meeting scheduled
- [ ] Community norms doc shared

## Accounts to Provision

| Service    | Who provisions | Notes                        |
|------------|---------------|------------------------------|
| GitHub     | Lead           | Add to the `members` team    |
| Slack      | Self-serve     | Link in welcome email        |
| Cloud env  | Infra team     | Submit ticket 2 days ahead   |

## First Week Goals

1. Read [Getting Started](/getting-started/)
2. Browse the [tag index](/tags/) to find relevant areas
3. Attend the weekly community call
